Message from the CEO





Our company's purpose - solving complex ISO & quality challenges - is what we aspire to do, in a comprehensive & sustainable context


Welcome, & thank you for visiting 4Z for Quality website. I am Dr. Tarek Elsherif, founder & chief executive officer. Our company's purpose - solving complex ISO conformity & quality management challenges - is what we aspire to do, in a comprehensive & sustainable context. This purpose has been clear, since we set out to establish a premium-quality consultancy services company.

I have always believed that when it comes to defining & communicating a company's vision & goals, conciseness is a virtue. We continuously focus on:
  • Quality – exceptional quality is what satisfies our customers, & it is the guarantee for the growth & retention of the customer base.
  • Service – excellent service is not just a theoretical concept, it is the practical way by which we develop customer loyalty, & maintain it.
  • Credibility – undebatable credibility is the driving force behind our growth in the short- & long-term.
  • Performance – peak performance is a commitment for us, to be fully prepared as individuals & as a company to do our best.
We put corporate moral responsibility as a top priority. That's why our reputation for integrity & honesty attracts excellent team members, great clients, & greater opportunities. It is the key to our success now, & in the future.

This is 4Z for Quality – a company with a vision we are proud of, & a bright future we are sure of!

As a company working in the field of ISO & quality, we have chosen "ISO made Easy" to be the slogan of 4Z for Quality, due to our prior knowledge of the stereotyped image of ISO standards & terminology, which may seem at first sight somewhat complicated, especially to the non-specialist. Following our slogan, we promise to simplify ISO standards as a concept & as a practice. We are committed to maximize satisfaction of your customers, by making quality & ISO standards a prevalent culture, not just a label, or a certificate.

I am confident that we will achieve leadership in our field, through our enthusiast & dedicated team members, & above all through our loyal & satisfied customers. This is 4Z for Quality – a company with a vision we are proud of, & a bright future we are sure of!

I invite you to explore our website, & learn more about everything we offer. I'm sure you'll see why we love what we do.

Sincerely,

Dr. Tarek Elsherif
CEO
